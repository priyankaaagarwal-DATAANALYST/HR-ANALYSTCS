# HR-ANALYSTCS
Interactive Power BI Dashboard for HR Analystics
📊 Training Performance Dashboard
🎯 Project Overview
A dynamic, interactive Power BI data visualization tool designed to track, analyze, and manage employee training completion statuses. This dashboard provides stakeholders with clear operational visibility into training progress across various home departments, specific courses, and regional locations to ensure compliance and workforce readiness.

🛠️ Tech Stack
The dashboard was built using the following tools and technologies:

📊 Power BI Desktop – Main data visualization platform used for report creation and interface design.

📂 Power Query – Data transformation and cleaning layer for reshaping, formatting dates, and preparing employee training records.

🧠 DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) – Used for calculated measures (e.g., total hours, completion percentages) and conditional logic.

📁 File Format – .pbix for development and .png for dashboard previews.

📂 Data Source
Source: Internal Human Resources & Learning Management System (LMS) database.

Structure: Tracking datasets covering training modules, employee IDs, department mapping, completion statuses (Completed, Pending, In Progress), and duration metrics (hours spent across different statuses).

💡 Features & Highlights
• Business Problem
Organizations often struggle to monitor training compliance across multiple departments and regional facilities. Without a centralized view, training coordinators and management cannot easily identify which departments are falling behind, which locations have outstanding requirements, or how many hours are currently tied up in pending or incomplete courses.

• Goal of the Dashboard
To deliver an interactive visual tool that:

Enables managers to audit training status instantly.

Benchmarks completion percentages across home departments and geographic locations.

Highlights overdue or delayed training modules to mitigate compliance risks.

• Walkthrough of Key Visuals
📈 Key KPIs (Top Panel)

🎛️ Interactive Filters & Slicers (Top Ribbon)
Global filters allowing users to drill down by Location, Home Department, Course, Position, Teammate Last Name, Status, and Date Range.

📊 Completion Status by Department (Horizontal Stacked Bar Chart)
Ranks and compares completion rates across core sectors.

Displays explicit progress benchmarks: Warehouse Management (100.00%), Transportation Management (91.67%), Sales (90.00%), and Transportation (82.76%).

📍 Training Performance By Location (Lower Left Progress Bar)
Isolates tracking for regional facilities, currently highlighting Graves Foods - Vinita, OK at an 88.61% completion rate.

⭕ Not Completed Risk Tracking (Lower Right Donut Chart)
A specialized risk visual with a 60+ Days threshold legend, pinpointing critical training delays that require immediate managerial intervention.

• Business Impact & Insights
Operational Accountability: Department heads can immediately see their target completion percentages and follow up with pending staff.

Resource Allocation: Tracking "Pending Duration" (253 hrs) helps management forecast how much operational time must be dedicated to upcoming training sessions.

Risk Mitigation: The "Not Completed" tracking mechanism prevents critical compliance gaps by flagging overdue tasks past the 60-day mark.
